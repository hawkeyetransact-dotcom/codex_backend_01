4. Customer A: Pharma Audit Demo Script

Demo objective: Show a complete supplier GMP audit lifecycle — from buyer creating a request to the auditor publishing a final report. Duration: ~20 minutes.

Module Pack: cGMP

Audit Management Supplier Quality CAPA Regulatory Intel RFQ Procurement AI Assistant Document Control Change Control Training

Demo Flow (3 actors, 12 steps)

Act 1: Buyer Initiates (login as buyer1.org@legacy.test)

1
Show FDA Intelligence Dashboard
Demonstrate regulatory intel — 331K inspections, supplier risk scores. "This is how you identify which suppliers need auditing."
/fda-dashboard
2
Create New Audit Request
Select supplier (Dr. Reddy's), product (Metformin HCl), site (Plant 2), set audit ETA. Show template selection for intimation letter.
/request-audit
3
Send Intimation Letter
Open the audit → Artifacts tab → INTIMATION_LETTER → "Send to Supplier". Show that supplierVisible flips to true.
/audits/[id]/artifacts

Act 2: Supplier Responds (login as dr.reddys@legacy.test)

4
Supplier Sees Audit
Show audit appearing in supplier's list. Open it. Review the intimation letter.
/audits (Audit Summary)
5
Accept Intimation
Click Accept. Calendar reservation created. Status moves to "Supplier accepted intimation".
/audits/[id]

Act 3: Buyer Assigns Auditor

6
Assign Auditor
Back as buyer. Assign auditor.one@legacy.test as LEAD. Show COI declaration requirement. Phase moves to PREP.
/audits/[id]

Act 4: Auditor Conducts Audit (login as auditor.one@legacy.test)

7
Review Scope & Send Questionnaire
Open artifacts → show SCOPE, AGENDA, EXECUTION_QUESTIONNAIRE artifacts. Select template. Send to supplier.
/audits/[id]/artifacts
8
Show Milestone Tracking
Open progress tab. Show 8-phase stepper, milestone completion status, SLA tracking.
/audits/[id]/progress

Act 5: Supplier Fills Questionnaire

9
Supplier Fills Responses
Login as supplier. Open questionnaire. Answer questions per category. Attach evidence. Submit.
/audits/[id]/questionnaire

Act 6: Findings & Closure

10
Auditor Reviews & Logs Findings
Login as auditor. Review responses. Log findings with classification (Critical/Major/Minor). Generate preliminary deficiency report.
/audits/[id]/artifacts
11
Supplier Submits CAPA Plan
Login as supplier. Open CAPA section. For each finding: root cause, corrective action, timeline. Submit.
/audits/[id]/artifacts
12
Final Report & Facility Outcome
Login as auditor. Generate final report (AI-assisted WHOPIR format). Set facility outcome: SATISFACTORY / CONDITIONAL / UNSATISFACTORY.
/audits/[id]/report
Key talking points: 8-phase workflow, multi-actor handoffs, AI-assisted report generation, 331K+ FDA regulatory records, real-time milestone tracking, artifact versioning with full audit trail.

5. Customer B: Pharma EQMS + Audit

Demo objective: Show the full quality management system — how deviations trigger CAPAs, how CAPAs drive document changes, how changes require retraining, plus the supplier audit flow. Duration: ~30 minutes.

All 13 Modules Active

Audit CAPA Doc Control Change Control Deviation/NC Complaints Training Risk Register Equipment/Cal Mgmt Review Supplier Quality Regulatory Intel AI Assistant

Demo Flow: "The Quality Event Cascade"

This demo tells a story: a temperature excursion in the warehouse triggers a chain reaction across 5 EQMS modules, showing how they're interconnected.

Scenario: Temperature Excursion in Cold Storage

1
Report Deviation
Go to Nonconformance Manager. Click "+ Report Deviation". Fill: title="Temperature excursion Cold Room A", category=ENVIRONMENTAL, classification=MAJOR, department=Warehouse. Show auto-numbering (DEV-2026-XXXX).
/nonconformance
2
Investigate Root Cause
Click "Investigate". Select RCA method=5-Why. Enter root cause: "HVAC backup relay failure — PM 6 months overdue". Root cause category=EQUIPMENT_FAILURE. Status moves to PENDING_DISPOSITION.
/nonconformance
3
Assess Impact
Click "Assess". Product quality impact: "Potential stability impact". Batch disposition: QUARANTINE. Show the impact assessment form.
/nonconformance
4
Log Customer Complaint (linked)
"Meanwhile, a customer noticed the issue." Go to Complaint Manager. Create: type=PRODUCT_QUALITY, severity=MAJOR, link to the deviation. Show cross-module traceability.
/complaint-manager
5
Check Equipment Record
"Let's check the HVAC unit." Go to Equipment/Calibration. Show that the last PM was overdue. Record a new calibration. Show calibration history.
/asset-management
6
Raise Change Control
"This needs a process change." Go to Change Controls. Create: type=PROCESS, risk=MEDIUM, description="Add dual-relay failover to all cold storage HVAC". Show approval workflow.
/change-controls
7
Update SOP (Document Control)
"The SOP needs updating." Go to Document Control. Create new version of the PM SOP. Show: DRAFT → review → approve → publish workflow. Point out requiresTrainingOnUpdate flag.
/document-control
8
Assign Training
"Updated SOP means retraining." Go to Training Management. Create: type=SOP_READ_AND_UNDERSTAND, trainee=Warehouse Team, linked to the document. Show competency assessment on completion.
/training
9
Risk Register Entry
"Log this in the risk register." Go to Risk Register. Show how the deviation, CAPA, and change control are all linked to a single risk item.
/risk-register
10
Management Review Dashboard
"In the next quarterly review, all of this data flows in." Go to Management Review. Create a review. Show how KPIs aggregate from all modules. Set QMS adequacy = ADEQUATE.
/management-review
11
Supplier Audit Flow (abbreviated)
"And when it's time to audit the supplier who provided the material..." Show the 8-phase audit flow from Customer A's demo (steps 2-12) in fast-forward.
/request-audit
Key talking points: Cross-module traceability (deviation → complaint → CAPA → change → document → training → risk → review), auto-numbering across all modules, ISO 9001:2015 and ICH Q10 compliance, single source of truth for quality records.

Demo Flow: "EHS Inspection + Quality Event"

Part 1: EHS Inspection (Module Pack: EHQS)

1
Create EHS Inspection Request
As Principal Company (buyer), create a new inspection. Select vendor/contractor, facility, inspection type. Note: vocabulary shows "EHS Inspection" not "Audit".
/request-audit
2
EHS Checklist
Inspector opens the execution questionnaire. EHQS module pack includes: hazard review, site walkthrough, environmental permits, incident history, emergency response assessment.
/audits/[id]/questionnaire
3
Log EHS Observations
Log observations: "Inadequate chemical spill containment at loading dock" (MAJOR), "Fire extinguisher inspection overdue" (MINOR). Show observation classification.
/audits/[id]/artifacts
4
Vendor Submits Corrective Actions
Vendor responds with corrective action plan: install containment berms (30 days), update fire extinguisher schedule (7 days).
/audits/[id]/artifacts

Part 2: EQMS Modules

5
Equipment Master
Show all plant equipment. Filter by calibration status. "This fire extinguisher is OVERDUE." Record calibration with certificate reference.
/asset-management
6
Report Deviation for Spill Containment Gap
Create deviation: category=ENVIRONMENTAL, classification=MAJOR. Investigate root cause. Link to the EHS inspection finding.
/nonconformance
7
Chain of Custody
Show CoC Tracker for chemical material batches. Track from receiving through processing to dispatch. "Every transfer is logged."
/coc-tracker
8
Document Control + Training
Update the spill response SOP. Assign safety retraining to warehouse team. Show training completion with competency sign-off.
/document-control → /training
Key talking points: EHS-specific inspection checklist (EHQS module pack), vocabulary customization ("Inspection" not "Audit"), environmental deviation tracking, chain of custody, equipment/calibration management, cross-module linking.
