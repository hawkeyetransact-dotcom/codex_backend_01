Cross-Module Automation (⚡ triggers)

Deviation → CAPA: When CAPA decision = required + autoCreateCapa = true, CAPA record auto-created and linked back to deviation.

Document Publish → Training: When requiresTrainingOnUpdate = true, SOP revision auto-assigns training to all affected users.

Equipment FAIL → Deviation: Calibration failure quarantines equipment and can trigger a deviation report.

Audit Findings → CAPA: Critical/Major findings from supplier audits auto-generate CAPA candidates.

Complaint → Regulatory: MDR/Safety complaints auto-flag for regulatory reporting (FDA MedWatch, EU Vigilance).

All Modules → KPIs: Management Review auto-aggregates counts from every module via GET /api/quality/kpis.

A Typical Day in the Life

6:00 AM — Cold storage alarm. Warehouse operator reports deviation in HawkEye (/nonconformance). Classification: MAJOR.

7:00 AM — QA Manager assesses impact. Batch quarantined. Root cause: HVAC relay failure. Equipment record updated (/asset-management).

8:00 AM — CAPA auto-created from deviation. Engineering raises change control for dual-relay failover (/change-controls).

9:00 AM — Customer calls about packaging issue. Complaint logged (/complaint-manager). Linked to same batch.

10:00 AM — PM SOP revised and published (/document-control). Training auto-assigned to warehouse team (/training).

2:00 PM — Risk register updated with new FMEA entry (/risk-register). RPN calculated.

Monthly — Management review pulls KPIs from all modules. QMS adequacy assessed. Action items tracked.

Quarterly — Supplier re-audit triggered based on risk score. 8-phase workflow begins.
