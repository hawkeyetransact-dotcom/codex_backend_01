Pharma tailwinds driving EQMS spend

EU GMP Annex 11 revision

Draft July 2025 tripled the chapter from 5→19 pages — prescriptive cybersecurity, AI validation, audit-trail lifecycle. Final mid-2026. Forces every EU-market pharma to re-validate or replace legacy systems. source

DSCSA enforcement live

May 27 2025 (wholesale) + Nov 27 2025 (large dispensers) ended stabilisation period. Package-level electronic traceability now mandatory in US supply chain. source

FDA AI-quality draft guidance (Jan 2025)

Risk-based credibility framework — AI now formally in scope of validated GxP systems. Pulls AI/LLM requirements into EQMS RFPs. source

CDMO consolidation + capacity

CDMO market USD 155.5B (2024) → USD 293.6B (2033) at 7.38% CAGR. Fujifilm's $3.2B Holly Springs site alone drives multi-client EQMS demand. GLP-1 + ADC + biosimilars accelerate. source

FDA QMSR effective Feb 2, 2026

Harmonises US medical-device reg with ISO 13485:2016, pulling combination-product pharma into device-QMS standards. source

Hawkeye's strategic moats

1. Auditor marketplace (RFQ → award)

The only EQMS with a built-in third-party auditor marketplace. CDMOs + mid-market buy audit capacity on-demand. RFQ + quote comparison + rating system. No competitor ships this.

2. Multi-industry from one codebase

Module config + vocabulary overlay → one platform, 7+ industry packs. MasterControl + Veeva are pharma-only; Qualio is life-sci-only; ETQ is multi-industry but pharma-weak. Hawkeye is the only platform architected for pharma-depth AND multi-vertical breadth.

3. AskHawk — cross-module AI assistant

17-route AI stack with tool-calling across audit/CAPA/evidence. Peers have per-module AI (Veeva Agents, TrackWise AI). Hawkeye's AskHawk bridges modules and takes actions, not just answers.

4. Redacted-view document model

Doc-control serves auditor-safe redacted views from the same source of truth. Rare primitive — enables confidential supplier audits without data leakage.

5. Public-signal supplier risk

FDA 483, warning letters, import alerts feed directly into supplier risk scores. Competitors require manual risk inputs; Hawkeye's is automated.

6. Immutable records by default

Hash-chain + dataIntegrityLog + ALCOA+ model built-in. On the roadmap: WORM anchoring + optional blockchain proof. Turns FDA integrity proof from a 6-week exercise to a single API call.
