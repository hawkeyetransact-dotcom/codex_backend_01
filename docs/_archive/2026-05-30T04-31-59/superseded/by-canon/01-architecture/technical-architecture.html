1. System Overview

HawkEye is a multi-tenant, role-based supply chain audit and quality management platform connecting buyers, suppliers, and auditors through a structured 8-phase workflow engine. The platform spans supplier qualification, audit lifecycle management, CAPA tracking, risk scoring, compliance monitoring, and document management.

Platform Actors

💼Buyer

Initiates audit requests, selects suppliers and auditors, tracks compliance across their supply chain, reviews final reports.

🏭Supplier

Responds to audit requests, submits questionnaires, provides evidence documents, files CAPA plans for findings.

🔎Auditor

Conducts audits, reviews supplier responses, logs findings, generates compliance reports, manages the assessment lifecycle.

⚙Tenant Admin

Manages organization settings, user roles, activated modules, and workflow configurations within their tenant.

🔒Platform Admin

Manages the entire platform: tenants, subscriptions, governance policies, global templates, and system settings.

Multi-Tenancy Model

Single-database, query-level isolation. Every document includes a tenant_id or tenantOrgId field. Middleware injects the tenant from the JWT. Queries always filter by tenant. Records with tenant_id=null are platform-global (shared templates, FDA data, standards).

9. AI & Intelligence Layer

🤖 AskHawk (Knowledge AI)

Document ingestion → vector embedding → KB chunks → intent routing → hybrid scoring → reranking → grounded responses with citations.

Models: KbArticle, KbChunk, HawkConversation, HawkUnanswered

⚡ Form Auto-Fill

Agentic autofill for signup, questionnaires, and evidence mapping. Profile import with LLM-driven field extraction from uploaded documents.

Services: agenticFormAutofill, profileImport, aiPrefill

📈 Risk Scoring Engine

11 sub-services: scoring v1/v2, buyer weighting, evidence trust, network exposure, trend analysis, improvements, breakdowns, reasons. Runs daily via cron at 2:30 AM.

Data: SupplierRiskMetrics, SupplierRiskEvent, SupplierRiskSnapshot

🌐 Public Intelligence

Syncs FDA inspections (331K+), citations (272K+), regulatory signals. Scheduled connector pulls data and matches to internal suppliers.

Source: FDA Data Dashboard (CSV), 10 public intel models
