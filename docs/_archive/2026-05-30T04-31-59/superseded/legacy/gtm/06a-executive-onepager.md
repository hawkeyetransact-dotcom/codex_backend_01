## What's distinctive

- **Marketplace-native, not a portal.** Suppliers have public profiles. Audit reports can be licensed by other buyers ($2-4k each). Your audit budget goes 5x further.
- **AI is in every workflow, not a side panel.** 12 agents wired into Audit / CAPA / Risk / Deviation / Complaint / Training / Change.
- **Vertical-pack architecture.** One workflow OS, packaged per vertical (pharma now, med-device Q4 2026, ISO 9001 Q1 2027). When you expand into a new vertical, you don't replatform.
- **No Salesforce tax.** Hawkeye runs on its own infrastructure — you're not paying for a Salesforce license you don't use.
- **India cost base.** Engineering and audit ops both run from India. Same compliance, half the price of US-built EQMS.
