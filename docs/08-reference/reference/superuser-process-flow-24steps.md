# Hawk-Eye Audit Management — Super User Defined Process (24 Steps)

Source: Google Sheets (latest super user defined process flow)

| Step | What | Precursor | Who | When | Whom | How | Why | Remarks |
|------|------|-----------|-----|------|------|-----|-----|---------|
| #001 | Auditee onboarding – Non-Technical Checklist | R&D request | Buyer Purchase Team | Initial Stage | Supplier Sales/Marketing | Email/Hawkeye/Supplier Portal Login | To enable onboarding on Buyer SCM/Finance/QA systems | |
| #002 | Auditee Due Diligence – Technical/Primary Checklist | #001 | Buyer QA/Regulatory | After Purchase decision to pursue further | Supplier QA, through their SCM | Formal Checklist execution – Email/HawkEye | To gather requisite document for provisional approval and sample sourcing | For Approvability once the trials are good |
| #003 | Sample procurement and feasibility/R&D trial | #002 | Buyer Purchase Team | As required by R&D | Supplier SCM | PO/Invoice | To establish product feasibility with material | PQ process |
| #004 | Provisional Approval | #002 | Buyer QA/Regulatory | Prior to sample procurement | Supplier QA, through their SCM | Certification/Hawkeye(?) | For documentation of due diligence in quality system | ID#001 to #004 not applicable for requalification audit. PQ process |
| #005 | Pre-Audit Communication | #001 | Buyer QA/Regulatory | Upon decision to perform further qualification & audit | Supplier QA, through their SCM | Email/Hawkeye | Primary coordination for the audit | |
| #006 | Due Diligence for Third Party auditor, Verification of COI & acceptance by Auditee | #001 | Buyer QA/Regulatory | Upon primary confirmation of audit slots | Supplier QA, through their SCM | Email/Hawkeye(?) | Finalizing the audit scheduling | |
| #007 | Audit Scheduling | #006 | Buyer QA/Regulatory | Upon agreement of audit slot and auditor | Supplier QA, through their SCM | Email/Hawkeye(?) | Formal Framework/communication for audit | |
| #008 | Pre-Audit Documentation request | #007 | Buyer QA/Regulatory/Auditor | Once audit is confirmed | Supplier QA, through their SCM | Email/Hawkeye(?) | To gather sufficient data to create | SMF, SOP List, Product info like Spec/STP, Stability, process flow |
| #009 | Pre-Audit Questionnaire | #008 | Auditor | After getting Pre-audit documents | Supplier QA, through their SCM | Email/Hawkeye(?) | To ascertain the status of site and design the execution/decide focus of audit | |
| #010 | Audit Agenda | #008 | Auditor | After getting Pre-audit documents | Supplier QA, through their SCM | Email/Hawkeye(?) | Provide a brief outline of audit process, whether on-site or offsite | |
| #011 | Audit Checklist – Standard verification | #009 | Buyer QA/Regulatory | After getting Pre-audit documents | Shared to Supplier QA if virtual audit/filled by Auditor if onsite | Email/Hawkeye(?) | To verify the complete standard requirements | Through aide-de-memoir |
| #012 | Audit Execution | #011 | Auditor | On scheduled dates | Auditee | Onsite/Offsite | Core process | |
| #013 | Client & Auditee Company presentation | #012 | Auditor & Buyer | At opening meeting | Auditor/Buyer/Supplier | E copy | For basic info & context of product/facility | |
| #014 | Closing meeting reporting | #012 | Auditor | At closing meeting | Auditee (Supplier) QA | Verbal communication/discussion/notes | To align auditee regarding identified/listed deficiency and any clarification | |
| #015 | Facility Certification/Communication | #014 | Auditor/Buyer QA | At Closing meeting or just afterwards | Auditee (Supplier) QA | Formal communication/email | To certify and endorse the continued compliance or approval or discontinuation | Options: 1. Approved pending CAPA plan if no Critical and <=5 Major 2. Conditionally approved pending specific action 3. Rejected & reaudit recommended |
| #016 | Deficiency Reporting | #014 | Auditor | Within a week of Audit | Supplier QA, through their SCM | Formal communication/email | To enable auditee to start remediation/compliance | Auditor normally consults with Buyer QA for endorsement for categorization |
| #017 | Deficiency Validation/Acceptance | #016 | Auditor & Buyer | Within one week of official Deficiency reporting | Supplier QA, through their SCM | Formal communication/email | To clarify/agree on the listed deficiencies | Sometimes Buyer QA is involved, sometimes this step is skipped |
| #018 | Audit Report | #017 | Auditor | Normally within 30 days from audit date | Supplier QA, through their SCM | Formal report | To have a formal documented report with all details | Main report |
| #019 | CAPA Plan | #018 | Supplier QA | Normally within 30 days from report date | Supplier QA, through their SCM | Signed report with CAPA plan and status/Target Date | To ascertain the compliance for the deficiencies | |
| #020 | Review & Acceptance/rejection of CAPA Plan | #019 | Auditor & Buyer QA | Normally within 7 days of CAPA plan submission | Supplier QA, through their SCM | Email communications/meeting sessions | To arrive at a decision whether CAPA plan is adequate to close the audit | Normally multiple iterations with multiple interactions |
| #021 | Audit Closure Certification | #020 | Auditor | After evaluation of CAPA plan | Supplier QA, through their SCM | Formal signed report | To formally close out the audit process and handover to Buyer | Auditor closes upon acceptance of Critical, Major and Other deficiencies |
| #022 | CAPA tracking/Follow up | #019 | Buyer QA/Regulatory/Supplier mgmt | As per target date | Supplier QA, through their SCM | Email/phone calls and evidence copies | Keep track of commitments by supplier and escalate as required | Third Party Auditor normally hands over monitoring to Buyer after closure |
| #023 | Supplier monitoring/rating | #021 | Buyer QA/Regulatory/Supplier mgmt | Normally monthly | Supplier QA, through their SCM | As per buyer SOP/format | Supplier Qualification formalities | |
| #024 | Follow up/requalification Audit Planning | #021 | Buyer QA/Regulatory/Supplier mgmt | Normally Yearly based on audit outcome and monitoring | Supplier QA, through their SCM | As per Buyer SOP/Risk assessment policy | Supplier Qualification formalities | |
