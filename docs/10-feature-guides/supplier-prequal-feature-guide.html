1. Module overview

ModuleSupplier Pre-Qualification
Pharma purposeScreen a new supplier before scheduling a full audit. Capture initial risk band, regulatory standards, product categories, checklist compliance. Output: APPROVED / CONDITIONALLY_APPROVED / REJECTED with validity window.
Compliance basisICH Q10 §2.7 · 21 CFR 211.84 (component qualification) · EU GMP Chapter 7 · ISO 13485 §7.4
Hawkeye routes/supplier-prequalification (frontend) · /api/supplier-prequalifications (backend)
Hawkeye model filebackend/src/models/SupplierPreQualificationModel.js
Tenant module flagmodules.SUPPLIER_QUALITY

Pre-quals follow DRAFT → SUBMITTED → UNDER_REVIEW → APPROVED / CONDITIONALLY_APPROVED / REJECTED. An APPROVED PQ becomes the basis for scheduling a full audit. CONDITIONALLY_APPROVED carries explicit conditions tracked separately. EXPIRED is auto-set when validUntil passes.

9. Known gaps + roadmap

  • Auto-EXPIRE scheduler — Cron flips status to EXPIRED when validUntil passes. HIGH
  • Auto-create audit request from APPROVED PQ — One-click 'Schedule Full Audit' button on APPROVED PQ. HIGH
  • AI Supplier-Intel button on the +Start dialog — Pre-fill the PQ with public signals before submission. MEDIUM
  • Conditions tracker module — CONDITIONALLY_APPROVED PQs need ongoing tracking; today conditions are free-text. MEDIUM
